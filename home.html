Refund & Cancellation Policy

No Refund for Delivered Services

Once services have been delivered or campaigns have been launched on Meta platforms, no refunds will be issued. This includes ad spend, creative development, and campaign setup fees.

Campaign Management

Once a campaign is live on Facebook or Instagram, the ad spend is managed by Meta and cannot be recovered. Sunkiran Dimex does not handle refunds for platform advertising costs.

Support for Issues

If you experience technical or service-delivery issues, our team is available to provide support and remediation options. Contact us within 24-48 hours of discovering the issue.

Cancellation Process

  • Contact Sunkiran Dimex to request campaign cancellation
  • Outstanding service fees remain due
  • Live campaigns can be paused by Meta at any time
  • No refunds are issued for partial campaign periods

Future Service Credits

In cases of service failure or dissatisfaction, we may offer account credits for future services at our discretion. Contact support@sunkiran.in to discuss options.

Policy Effective Date
