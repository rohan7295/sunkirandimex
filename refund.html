Refund Policy

Last updated · 2025  ·  Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra

Overview

At Sunkiran Dimex, we are committed to delivering high-quality digital marketing services. Due to the nature of our services, all payments made are generally non-refundable, except under the specific conditions outlined below.

✅

Eligibility for Refunds

Refunds may be considered only in the following cases:

  • If a project has not been initiated after payment confirmation
  • If there is a duplicate payment made by the client
  • If Sunkiran Dimex is unable to deliver the agreed service due to internal limitations
🚫

Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be provided under the following circumstances:

  • Once the project, campaign setup, or service has been initiated or delivered
  • Dissatisfaction due to campaign performance, as results depend on external factors (e.g., audience behavior, platform algorithms, competition)
  • Delays caused by the client, including lack of communication, approvals, or required materials
  • Changes in client requirements after the project has started
📋

Cancellation Policy

  • Clients may request cancellation before the project begins
  • Once work has commenced, cancellation requests will not be eligible for a refund
⏱️

Processing of Refunds

  • Approved refunds will be processed within 7–10 business days
  • Refunds will be issued via the original payment method, unless otherwise agreed
