About Sunkiran Dimex

Who We Are

Sunkiran Dimex is a registered marketing agency based in Pimpri-Chinchwad, Maharashtra. We specialize in Facebook and Instagram advertising, providing end-to-end campaign management services for businesses across India.

Our mission is to help businesses improve their online presence through ethical, transparent, and compliant digital marketing strategies. We believe in building long-term partnerships with our clients based on trust, results, and continuous improvement.

What We Do

  • Meta Ads Management - Professional campaign setup and optimization
  • Audience Targeting - Precision targeting for maximum reach and engagement
  • Creative & Copy Development - Engaging ad content that converts
  • Lead Generation - Strategic campaigns for quality lead capture
  • Campaign Tracking - Real-time analytics and detailed performance reports
  • Full Marketing Support - Dedicated assistance and strategy optimization

Our Commitment

We operate using transparent, secure, and policy-compliant methods. We respect your data, follow all Meta platform guidelines, and maintain the highest standards of ethical advertising practices.

Every campaign we run is designed with your business goals in mind, backed by data-driven insights and continuous optimization to ensure you get the best possible results.
